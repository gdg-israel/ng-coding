ng-coding Rules

  • The goal is to contribute to open-source. Play fair!
  • Pick and issue from the Contributions List, then click the "Work on this" button.
  • When you finish working on an issue, you will be gain score, as determined by the ng-coding mentors.
  • All data is open and public on GitHub.
